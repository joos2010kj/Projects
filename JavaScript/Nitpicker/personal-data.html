INSTRUCTIONS:



Enter the information of the course you are interested in taking but has no seat left in ALARM SETUP box.
The section number (e.g. 0101) is optional, but please make sure to specify the following three:
- Course ID (e.g. CMSC422)
- Season (e.g. Spring)
- Year (e.g. 2020)

Once you have entered the information, you will see the course in HISTORY box. If you don't see, then please refresh the page.

Now, Nitpicker will be checking all the courses you have in your HISTORY box and e-mail you (to the one you signed up with) as soon as an open seat is found for the course. Please keep in mind that Nitpicker has the name "Nitpicker" because it will be pestering you every 10 minutes with e-mails once it finds an open seat until you tell it to STOP.

Therefore, once you have received an email from Nitpicker, I advise you to register the course as soon as possible and then follow the steps below to command it to stop:
1) If you take a look at each registered course inside HISTORY box, you will see that each course has an INDEX value (e.g. INDEX: 3).
2) Find the course you would like to remove from HISTORY box, and remember the INDEX value of the course.
3) Go to REMOVAL box, and enter the number (e.g. 3), and hit submit.
Then you will see the course disappear from HISTORY. If somehow everything goes missing in HISTORY box, please refresh the page.
